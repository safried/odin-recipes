Peanut Sauce

Description

A dear friend of mine shared this recipe with me and it's incredibly delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 lb blanched unsalted peanuts
  • 2 walnut sized pieces of ginger, minced
  • 2/3 jar mango chutney
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • salt to taste
  • chili (optional)
  • water (for thickness)

Steps

  1. Mix soft ingredients (not including water) in a blender
  2. Add peanuts and some water and blend
  3. Mixture will be thick. Add a splash of water at a time until desired consistency is reached
  4. Heat mixture over the stove until warm
    5. Enjoy
