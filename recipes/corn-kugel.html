Corn Kugel(Casserole)

Description

Every year for as long as I can remember, my Aunt has made this for Thanksgiving. It's basically a kosher corn casserole and always a crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup margarine or oil
  • 2 TBSP sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
    • alternatively could use coffee rich or evaporated milk or regular milk with a teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 cans creamed corn

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Melt margarine if using
  3. Add all ingredients EXCEPT corn to a bowl and mix until well combined
  4. Add corn to the mixture and stir until combined
  5. Pour into a greased pan
  6. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes
  7. Use a toothpick to check if it's done cooking (if it comes out clean, the dish is done)
  8. Enjoy at any temperature

Notes

  • The finished dish should be slightly pulled away from the sides of the pan
